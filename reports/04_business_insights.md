# Business Insights

This document translates EDA findings into actionable insights for planning, exports, and subsidy decisions.

## Aggregate Growth and Volatility

- Year-over-year growth rate of total production analyzed
- Crop-wise volatility (std deviation) calculated
- Top risky crops identified by high volatility

## Recent Production and Reliability

- Average production over last 3 years computed per crop
- Reliable crops: low volatility + strong recent production
- Risky crops: high volatility

**Reliable crops**
- Paddy, Coffee, Coconut

**Risky crops**
- Pepper, Clove, Nutmeg & Mace, Cinnamon, Cashew, Cocoa, Rubber

## Observing Growth Patterns

- Positive growth observed in 2007, 2010, 2012
- Sharp drop in 2011
- Single-year spikes/dips flagged for follow-up

## Policy and Business Recommendations

- Prioritize reliable crops for logistics, storage, and export
- Conditional subsidies or insurance for risky crops
- Consider processing or value-added chains for risky crops
- Pilot interventions for risky crops with long-term potential

**Conclusion**

- The analysis guides strategic planning in Sri Lankan agriculture
- Helps optimize production, export, and subsidy allocation decisions
